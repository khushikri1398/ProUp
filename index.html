Connecting Startups with Investors

The Problem

Startups often struggle to find the right investors. Many fail due to lack of funding and connections. Investors also find it hard to discover startups that match their interests, resulting in missed opportunities.

Solution: ProUp

ProUp is a mobile app that connects startups with potential investors using AI-powered matchmaking, in-app messaging, and growth tools. Our platform helps startups showcase their potential to the right investors, while providing investors with the best opportunities to grow their portfolio.

Key Features

AI-based Matchmaking

Personalized matchmaking using AI to connect startups with investors based on interests, needs, and goals.

Investor Dashboard

Advanced filters and analytics to help investors easily find startups that match their criteria.

In-app Messaging

Facilitate communication between startups and investors directly within the platform, fostering meaningful connections.

Meeting Scheduler

Built-in scheduling tool to arrange meetings between startups and investors at convenient times.
